Link to privacy policy of third party service providers used by the app

* [Firebase Crashlytics](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/)

 

In our application we use Google Crashlytics service in order to get information about crashes in the application.

 

This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information.

 

This information will be sent **ONLY** when crash occurs and does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include next data:

 

* Smartphone manufacturer and model;

> Example: Samsung S9 or iPhone XR.

 

* Mobile OS version;

> Example: Android 9 or iOS 13.3.1.

 

* Free RAM and ROM space;

> Example: Free RAM 64.94 MB or Free space 685.41 MB.

 

* Phone screen orientation;

> Example: Vertical or Horizontal.

 

* Application version;

> Example: BeadingColors 1.0.0.

 

This information allows us to group issues by phone model, OS version, RAM and ROM specifications and reproduce them in order to fix and improve our application.

 

Also, we use Google Analytics for Firebase service to know how useful our application for you.

 

We use next data from analytics:

 

* Session length;

> Example: average application usage - 15 minutes.

 

* Country/Region;

> Example: Russia.

 

We do not track any custom events from application.

 

Also, Google Analytics for Firebase may collect other unpersonalized information, so please read it by reference we mentioned above.

 

We may use and store this information in aggregated and anonymized form so that it is not associated with individual end users and does not include personal information. We will not use identifiable personal information without your consent.
